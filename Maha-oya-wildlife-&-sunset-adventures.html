Maha Oya Wildlife & Sunset Adventure

  1. Home
  2. Negombo Boat Tours
  3. Maha Oya Wildlife & Sunset Adventure

Take a trip along the idyllic and untouched beauty of the Maha Oya River on this 3-4 hour boat tour. Ideal for nature enthusiasts, birders, photographers, and anyone looking to get away into the peace of Negombo's natural environment, the Maha Oya Wildlife & Sunset Adventure provides a rare combination of wildlife spotting, stunning scenic vistas, and a relaxed riverside outing that will captivate you.

What You’ll See & Experience

Wildlife Sightings:

The Maha Oya River is a haven for birdwatchers and nature lovers. As you cruise along, keep your eyes peeled for some of the region’s most fascinating wildlife:

Red Kingfisher: This vibrant bird, with its striking colors, is a true gem of the river. Spotting one perched on a branch or diving into the water is a moment you’ll want to capture.

Red-wattled Lapwing: With its distinctive red wattle and loud calls, this bird is hard to miss. It’s a treat for birdwatchers and adds a unique charm to the river’s ecosystem.

Eagles: Watch in awe as majestic eagles soar high above the river and estuary, their sharp eyes scanning the waters below.

Diverse Birdlife: From herons to egrets, the riverbanks are alive with the sights and sounds of countless bird species.

Scenic Views

The Maha Oya River isn’t just about wildlife—it’s also about breathtaking landscapes.

Estuary Sunset: As the day winds down, prepare to be mesmerized by the estuary sunset. The sky transforms into a canvas of oranges, pinks, and purples, reflecting on the calm waters. It’s a sight that will stay with you long after the tour ends.

Sea Views: At the point where the river meets the sea, you’ll be treated to stunning views of the ocean. The contrast between the river’s calm waters and the sea’s waves is a sight to behold.

Riverside Experience

The tour isn’t just about what you see from the boat—it’s also about immersing yourself in the riverside environment.

Explore the Riverbank: Step off the boat and take a stroll along the lush riverbank. The greenery, the sounds of nature, and the fresh air make it a perfect spot to relax and reconnect with nature.

Water Plant View Point: Visit the Water Plant View Point for panoramic views of the river and its surroundings. It’s a great spot for photos and offers a unique perspective of the river’s ecosystem.

Why You’ll Love This Tour

This tour is perfect for anyone who loves nature, wildlife, and stunning landscapes. Whether you’re traveling solo, with family, or with friends, the Maha Oya Wildlife & Sunset Adventure offers something for everyone. It’s a chance to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and immerse yourself in the beauty of Sri Lanka’s natural world
