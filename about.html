About us

Welcome to Negambo Boat Tour Experience Company, your gateway to exploring the breathtaking beauty of Negombo’s waterways. We specialize in offering unforgettable boat tours that showcase the region’s rich wildlife, stunning sunsets, and serene landscapes. Whether you’re a nature enthusiast, a birdwatcher, or simply seeking a peaceful escape, our expertly crafted tours provide an immersive experience into the heart of Negombo’s natural wonders. With a focus on sustainability and customer satisfaction, we ensure every journey with us is both memorable and eco-friendly.

Why Choose Our Fleet?

  • Expert Guides: Knowledgeable and friendly guides to enhance your experience.
  • Diverse Tour Options: From birdwatching to sunset cruises, we cater to all interests.
  • Eco-Friendly Practices: Committed to preserving the natural beauty of Negombo
  • Unforgettable Memories: Stunning views, wildlife sightings, and serene moments guaranteed
