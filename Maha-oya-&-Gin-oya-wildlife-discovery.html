Maha oya & Gin oya wildlife discovery

  1. Home
  2. Negombo Boat Tours
  3. Maha oya & Gin oya wildlife discovery

If you’re looking for a shorter but equally captivating experience, the Maha Oya & Gin Oya Wildlife Discovery is the perfect choice. This 2-hour tour takes you through two of Negombo’s most beautiful rivers, offering a mix of wildlife sightings, scenic views, and a touch of adventure.

What You’ll See & Experience

Wildlife Sightings:

The Maha Oya and Gin Oya rivers are teeming with life, and this tour gives you a front-row seat to some of the region’s most fascinating creatures.

Red Kingfisher: This colorful bird is a highlight of the tour. Watch as it darts across the water, its vibrant feathers catching the sunlight.

Cormorants These sleek, black birds are often seen perched on rocks or swimming in the river. Watching them dive for fish is a fascinating sight.

Bat Forest: One of the most unique aspects of this tour is the bat forest. As you approach, you’ll see hundreds of bats hanging from the trees, creating a surreal and unforgettable scene.

Egrets: These elegant birds, with their pure white feathers, are a common sight along the riverbanks. Watching them wade through the water is a peaceful and calming experience.

Monitor Lizards: Keep an eye out for these fascinating reptiles as they bask in the sun or scurry along the riverbanks.

Scenic Views

The tour isn’t just about wildlife—it’s also about the stunning landscapes that surround the rivers.

Estuary Sunset: As the day winds down, prepare to be mesmerized by the estuary sunset. The sky transforms into a canvas of oranges, pinks, and purples, reflecting on the calm waters. It’s a sight that will stay with you long after the tour ends.

Sea Views: At the point where the river meets the sea, you’ll be treated to stunning views of the ocean. The contrast between the river’s calm waters and the sea’s waves is a sight to behold.

Why You’ll Love This Tour

This tour is ideal for those who want a shorter but immersive experience. It’s perfect for families, couples, and solo travelers who want to connect with nature and enjoy the beauty of Negombo’s rivers. The combination of wildlife sightings, scenic views, and a touch of adventure makes this tour a must-do for anyone visiting the area.
